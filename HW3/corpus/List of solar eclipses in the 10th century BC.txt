this is a list of solar eclipses in the 10th century bc during the period 1000 to 901 bc there were 226 solar eclipses of which 84 were partial 75 were annular one non-central 61 were total three non-central and 6 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four occurring in 5 different years 996 bc 960 bc 949 bc 931 bc and 913 bc
