a partial solar eclipse occurred on may 11 1975 a solar eclipse occurs when the moon passes between earth and the sun thereby totally or partly obscuring the image of the sun for a viewer on earth a partial solar eclipse occurs in the polar regions of the earth when the center of the moons shadow misses the earth
