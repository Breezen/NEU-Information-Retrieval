this is a list of solar eclipses in the 10th century during the period 901 to 1000 there were 227 solar eclipses of which 76 were partial 84 were annular one non-central 66 were total one non-central and 1 was hybrid the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four in 911 922 940 958 and 991 dates are given using the julian calendar
