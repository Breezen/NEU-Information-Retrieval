this is a list of solar eclipses in the 3rd century bc during the period 300 to 201 bc there were 226 solar eclipses of which 83 were partial 62 were annular one non-central 57 were total two non-central and 24 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four in 298 bc 233 bc and 211 bc
