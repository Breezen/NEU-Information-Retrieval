this is a list of solar eclipses in the 14th century bc during the period 1400 to 1301 bc there were 234 solar eclipses of which 76 were partial 84 were annular one non-central 68 were total and 6 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four occurring in 6 different years 1372 bc 1354 bc 1336 bc 1332 bc 1318 bc and 1307 bc
