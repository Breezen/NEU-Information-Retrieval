a partial solar eclipse took place on february 15 2018 a solar eclipse occurs when the moon passes between earth and the sun thereby totally or partly obscuring the image of the sun for a viewer on earth a partial solar eclipse occurs in the polar regions of the earth when the center of the moons shadow misses the earth
the eclipse took place before sunset in the southern cone of south america
