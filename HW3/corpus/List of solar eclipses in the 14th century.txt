this is a list of solar eclipses in the 14th century during the period 1301 to 1400 there were 229 solar eclipses of which 76 were partial 75 were annular three non-central 54 were total and 24 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four occurring in 7 different years 1302 1309 1320 1331 1349 1367 and 1396 dates are given using the julian calendar
