this is a list of solar eclipses in the 11th century bc during the period 1100 to 1001 bc there were 238 solar eclipses of which 79 were partial 91 were annular two non-central 68 were total one non-central and none were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was five in 1090 bc and three months august 1036 bc july 1025 bc and june 1014 bc had two eclipses
