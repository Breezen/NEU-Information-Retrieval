this is a list of solar eclipses in the 15th century during the period 1401 to 1500 there were 222 solar eclipses of which 77 were partial 65 were annular three non-central 61 were total one non-central and 19 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four occurring in 6 different years 1414 1432 1443 1461 1479 and 1497 dates are given using the julian calendar
