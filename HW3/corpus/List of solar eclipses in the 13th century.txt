this is a list of solar eclipses in the 13th century during the period 1201 to 1300 there were 246 solar eclipses of which 87 were partial 81 were annular one non-central 60 were total and 18 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was five in 1255 and one month march 1215 had two eclipses dates are given using the julian calendar
