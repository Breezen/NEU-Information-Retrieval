this is a list of solar eclipses in the 8th century bc during the period 800 to 701 bc there were 234 solar eclipses of which 79 were partial 88 were annular 64 were total and 3 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four occurring in 5 different years 750 bc 732 bc 725 bc 714 bc and 710 bc
