this is a list of solar eclipses in the 12th century bc during the period 1200 to 1101 bc there were 252 solar eclipses of which 93 were partial 89 were annular 63 were total and 7 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was five in 1155 bc and two months october 1123 bc and september 1112 bc had two eclipses
