this is a list of solar eclipses in the 7th century during the period 601 to 700 there were 251 solar eclipses of which 90 were partial 90 were annular one non-central 67 were total and 4 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was five in 604 and 669 and two months april 618 and march 629 had two eclipses dates are given using the julian calendar
