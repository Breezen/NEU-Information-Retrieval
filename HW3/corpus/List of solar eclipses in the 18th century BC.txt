this is a list of solar eclipses in the 18th century bc during the period 1800 to 1701 bc there were 254 solar eclipses of which 95 were partial 74 were annular one non-central 64 were total one non-central and 21 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was five in 1788 bc and 1741 bc
