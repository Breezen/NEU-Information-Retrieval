this is a list of solar eclipses in the 9th century during the period 801 to 900 there were 222 solar eclipses of which 78 were partial 74 were annular two non-central 64 were total two non-central and 6 were hybrids the greatest number of eclipses in one year was four in 810 828 875 and 893 dates are given using the julian calendar
